## Overview

The Point of Sale (POS) interface is the heart of Karma POS, allowing you to quickly process customer transactions with barcode scanning, product search, and flexible payment options.

## Opening the POS Interface

Navigate to the POS module from your dashboard. The interface opens with:
- An empty cart ready for items
- Barcode input field (automatically focused)
- Product search options
- Customer selector

<Tip>
The barcode input field is always auto-focused after actions, so you can scan items continuously without clicking.
</Tip>

## Adding Products to Cart

You have multiple ways to add products to a sale:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Scan Barcode">
    Simply scan the product barcode with your scanner. The system automatically:
    - Searches for the product by barcode
    - Adds it to the cart
    - Reserves stock if inventory management is enabled
    - Refocuses the input for the next scan
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Manual Barcode Entry">
    Type the barcode in the input field and press Enter.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Search by Product Code or Name">
    - Press **F2** to open the search dialog
    - Enter product code or name
    - Select the product from results
    - Click to add to cart
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Product Code Search">
    Use the product code field to search by SKU or product name. The system performs case-insensitive matching.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Cart Items

### Adjusting Quantities

<Steps>
  <Step title="Increase Quantity">
    - Modify the quantity field for any cart item
    - The system validates stock availability in real-time
    - Stock is automatically reserved
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Decrease Quantity">
    - Reduce the quantity value
    - Reserved stock is released back to inventory
    - Items with zero quantity are automatically removed
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
If stock is insufficient, you'll see a warning: "Solo hay X unidades más disponibles" and the quantity will revert to the previous valid amount.
</Warning>

### Applying Discounts

- **Product-level discounts**: Products with `hasDiscount` enabled automatically show discounted prices
- **Manual discounts**: Edit discount percentage in the payment dialog
- **General discount**: Apply a percentage discount to the entire cart in the payment screen

### Removing Products

Click the remove icon next to any product. Reserved stock is immediately released.

## Selecting a Customer

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Customer Selector">
    Click the customer selection dropdown or button in the POS interface.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Customer">
    - Search by name, NIT, or document number
    - Select from the list
    - Customer information populates automatically
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Default Customer">
    If no customer is selected, the sale processes as "Consumidor Final" (final consumer).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Processing the Payment

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Payment Dialog">
    Press **F3** or click the payment button when you're ready to checkout.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Sale Details">
    The payment dialog shows:
    - All cart items with quantities and prices
    - Gross subtotal (before discounts)
    - Item-level discounts
    - General discount field
    - Tax calculation (if configured)
    - Final total
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Payment Information">
    - Select payment method (cash, card, transfer, financed, other)
    - Enter payment amount
    - For financed payments, specify number of months
    - Use "Fill Exact Amount" to auto-complete remaining balance
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Split Payments (Optional)">
    Click "Add Payment Method" to split payment across multiple methods:
    - Cash + Card
    - Multiple cards
    - Any combination of available methods
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Delivery Information (Optional)">
    Toggle "Is Delivery" and fill in:
    - Address
    - City and State
    - ZIP code
    - Phone number
    - Delivery notes
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete the Sale">
    Press **Enter** or click "Process Payment" when total payments equal the sale total.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Completing the Sale

After processing payment:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Invoice Generation">
    The system automatically:
    - Generates a unique invoice number (e.g., FV-001)
    - Updates inventory levels
    - Records the transaction
    - Stores customer purchase history
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Print Invoice">
    The invoice dialog appears with:
    - Complete sale details
    - Print button
    - Option to print multiple copies (configured in settings)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Next Transaction">
    The POS automatically:
    - Clears the completed cart
    - Creates a new invoice if no other carts exist
    - Refocuses the barcode input
    - Keeps you ready for the next customer
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Multi-Invoice Management

Handle multiple customers simultaneously:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create New Invoice">
    Press **F5** to create a new invoice/cart while keeping the current one.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Switch Between Invoices">
    Click on any invoice tab to switch between customers.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Process Independently">
    Each invoice maintains its own:
    - Product list
    - Customer selection
    - Payment methods
    - Stock reservations
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Keyboard Shortcuts

Speed up your workflow with these shortcuts:

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| **F2** | Open product search dialog |
| **F3** | Open payment dialog |
| **F4** | Process sale (when payment complete) |
| **F5** | Create new invoice |
| **Enter** | Confirm payment (in payment dialog) |
| **Escape** | Close current dialog |

## Stock Management During Sales

### Real-Time Reservation

When you add products with `manageStock` enabled:

1. **Stock Validation**: System checks available stock across warehouses
2. **Reservation**: Stock is temporarily reserved with a unique `facturaTempId`
3. **Prevention**: Other users cannot sell reserved stock
4. **Release**: Stock is released if:
   - Quantity is reduced
   - Product is removed from cart
   - Invoice is cleared or cancelled

### Products Without Stock Management

Products with `manageStock: false` can be added without quantity limits.

## Handling Errors

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Product Not Found">
    - Verify the barcode is correct
    - Check if product exists in inventory
    - Ensure product is active
    - Try searching by name or code
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Insufficient Stock">
    - Warning displays available quantity
    - Reduce cart quantity to available amount
    - Check other warehouses for stock
    - Consider accepting a backorder (if enabled)
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Payment Incomplete">
    Error: "Verifica los montos"
    - Ensure payment total equals sale total
    - Use "Fill Exact Amount" button
    - Check for calculation errors in split payments
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="No Products in Cart">
    Warning: "Agrega productos antes de procesar"
    - Add at least one product before attempting payment
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Clearing an Invoice

To cancel a sale in progress:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Clear/Cancel">
    Use the clear button on the invoice.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Stock Release">
    All reserved stock is released back to inventory via `cancelInvoiceAll()` API call.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Invoice Removal">
    The invoice is removed from the active list. If it was the last invoice, a new empty one is created.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common Workflows

### Quick Cash Sale

1. Scan products
2. Press F3 (payment)
3. Enter cash amount
4. Press Enter
5. Print invoice

### Sale with Customer and Discount

1. Select customer first
2. Add products to cart
3. Open payment (F3)
4. Apply general discount if needed
5. Enter payment method
6. Complete sale

### Split Payment

1. Add all products
2. Open payment dialog (F3)
3. Enter first payment amount (e.g., cash)
4. Click "Add Payment Method"
5. Select second method (e.g., card)
6. Enter remaining amount
7. Process payment

### Multiple Customers

1. Start first customer's sale
2. Press F5 to create new invoice
3. Serve second customer
4. Switch between invoice tabs as needed
5. Complete each sale independently

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Barcode Scanner Not Working">
    - Check USB connection
    - Verify scanner settings in configuration
    - Test scanner in a text editor
    - Ensure barcode input has focus
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Prices Incorrect">
    - Verify product price in inventory
    - Check for active promotions
    - Ensure correct warehouse selected
    - Review discount configurations
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Invoice Not Printing">
    - Check printer connection
    - Verify printer settings (port, paper width)
    - Review printer configuration in settings
    - Test with system print dialog
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
