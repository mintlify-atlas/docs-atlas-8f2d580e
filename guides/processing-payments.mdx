## Overview

Karma POS supports flexible payment processing with multiple payment methods, split payments, and automatic change calculation. This guide covers everything you need to know about handling payments efficiently.

Karma POS supports five payment types:

  <Card title="🏦 Transfer" icon="building-columns">

## Opening the Payment Dialog

After adding products to the cart, open the payment screen:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Keyboard Shortcut">
    Press **F3** to instantly open the payment dialog.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Button Click">
    Click the **Payment** or **Checkout** button in the POS interface.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Automatic Focus">
    The first payment amount field is automatically focused for quick entry.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding the Payment Screen

The payment dialog displays:

### Sale Summary

- **Cart Items**: List of all products with quantities and prices
- **Gross Subtotal**: Total before any discounts
- **Detail Discounts**: Sum of product-level discounts
- **General Discount**: Percentage discount applied to entire sale
- **Subtotal**: Net amount after discounts, before tax
- **Tax (IVA)**: Calculated tax amount (if configured)
- **Total**: Final amount due

### Payment Information

- **Payment Method Selector**: Dropdown to choose payment type
- **Amount Field**: Input for payment amount
- **Difference Display**: Shows remaining balance or overpayment
- **Add Payment Method Button**: For split payments

### Optional Fields

- **Customer Selector**: Choose or confirm customer
- **Delivery Information**: Address and delivery details
- **Notes**: Special instructions or comments

## Processing Cash Payments

<Steps>
  <Step title="Verify Total">
    Confirm the total amount displayed at the bottom of the screen.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Cash Method">
    The payment type defaults to **Cash (💵 Efectivo)**. Leave it selected.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Amount Received">
    Type the amount the customer gave you:
    - Enter exact amount if paying precisely
    - Enter larger amount if customer is paying with larger bills
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Quick Fill Option">
    Click **Fill Exact Amount** button to auto-fill the exact total owed.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Calculate Change">
    The **Difference** field shows:
    - **Positive number**: Change to give back to customer
    - **Zero**: Exact payment
    - **Negative number**: Still owe this amount
    
    Example: 
    - Total: $50.00
    - Amount Received: $100.00
    - Difference: +$50.00 (give $50 change)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete Payment">
    When difference is ≥ $0.00:
    - Press **Enter** on keyboard
    - Or click **Process Payment** button
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
The system prevents processing if payment is incomplete (difference < $0). You must receive at least the total amount due.
</Tip>

## Processing Card Payments

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Card Method">
    Click the payment type dropdown and select **💳 Tarjeta (Card)**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Process Card Transaction">
    Use your external card terminal to process the payment:
    - Swipe, insert, or tap the customer's card
    - Wait for approval
    - Print card receipt if required
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Approved Amount">
    Type the exact amount approved by the card terminal.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete Transaction">
    Press **Enter** or click **Process Payment**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
Karma POS records the payment but doesn't directly integrate with card terminals. You must process the card separately and enter the approved amount.
</Info>

## Processing Bank Transfers

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Transfer Method">
    Choose **🏦 Transferencia (Transfer)** from the payment type dropdown.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Receive Transfer">
    - Provide customer with your bank account details
    - Wait for transfer confirmation
    - Or accept transfer reference number if already sent
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Transfer Amount">
    Input the transferred amount in the payment field.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Process Payment">
    Complete the sale. You can add the transfer reference in notes if needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Processing Financed Payments

For installment plans:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Financed Method">
    Choose **📅 Financiado (Financed)** from the payment dropdown.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Total Financed Amount">
    Input the amount to be financed.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Number of Months">
    A dropdown appears with options:
    - 3 months
    - 6 months
    - 9 months
    - 12 months
    - 18 months
    - 24 months
    
    Select the agreed-upon financing term.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete Transaction">
    Process the payment. The system records:
    - Total amount
    - Payment type (financed)
    - Number of installments
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Karma POS records the financing details but doesn't automatically create payment schedules or send reminders. Use a separate system for managing installment collections.
</Warning>

## Split Payments

Handle payments across multiple methods:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter First Payment">
    - Select payment method (e.g., Cash)
    - Enter amount for first payment
    - The **Difference** shows remaining balance
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Another Payment Method">
    Click **Add Payment Method** button. A new payment row appears.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Second Method">
    - Choose the second payment type (e.g., Card)
    - Enter the amount for this method
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Repeat if Needed">
    Continue adding payment methods until:
    ```
    Total Payments = Sale Total
    Difference = $0.00
    ```
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Remove Payment Method (if needed)">
    Click the **X** or remove icon next to any payment row to delete it.
    
    <Info>You must keep at least one payment method. The last remaining method cannot be removed.</Info>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Process Combined Payment">
    When total payments equal sale total, press **Enter** or click **Process Payment**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Split Payment Examples

#### Example 1: Cash + Card

**Sale Total**: $150.00

1. **Payment 1**: Cash - $100.00
2. Click "Add Payment Method"
3. **Payment 2**: Card - $50.00
4. Total Payments = $150.00 ✓
5. Process payment

#### Example 2: Financed + Cash Down Payment

**Sale Total**: $1,000.00

1. **Payment 1**: Cash - $200.00 (down payment)
2. Click "Add Payment Method"
3. **Payment 2**: Financed - $800.00 (12 months)
4. Total Payments = $1,000.00 ✓
5. Process payment

#### Example 3: Multiple Cards

**Sale Total**: $300.00

1. **Payment 1**: Card (Visa) - $150.00
2. Click "Add Payment Method"
3. **Payment 2**: Card (MasterCard) - $150.00
4. Total Payments = $300.00 ✓
5. Process payment

## Applying Discounts

### Product-Level Discounts

Apply discounts to individual items:

<Steps>
  <Step title="View Current Discount">
    Each cart item shows its current discount percentage.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Edit Item Discount">
    In the payment dialog:
    - Find the product in the cart list
    - Edit the discount percentage field
    - Valid range: 0% to 100%
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Automatic Calculation">
    The system recalculates:
    - Item total
    - Detail discount sum
    - Subtotal
    - Final total
  </Step>
</Steps>

### General Discount

Apply a percentage discount to the entire sale:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate General Discount Field">
    Find the **General Discount** input in the payment summary section.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Percentage">
    Type a discount percentage (e.g., 10 for 10% off).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Automatic Recalculation">
    The system updates:
    ```
    Base = Gross Subtotal - Detail Discounts
    General Discount Amount = Base × (General Discount % ÷ 100)
    Net Subtotal = Base - General Discount Amount
    Tax = Net Subtotal × Tax Rate
    Total = Net Subtotal + Tax
    ```
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
General discount applies to the subtotal after product-level discounts are applied, not the original gross amount.
</Info>

## Change Calculation

Karma POS automatically calculates change:

### How It Works

```
Difference = Total Payments - Sale Total
```

- **Difference > 0**: Give this amount as change
- **Difference = 0**: Exact payment (no change)
- **Difference < 0**: Customer still owes this amount

### Example Calculations

| Sale Total | Amount Received | Difference | Action |
|------------|----------------|------------|--------|
| $47.50 | $50.00 | +$2.50 | Give $2.50 change |
| $123.00 | $123.00 | $0.00 | No change needed |
| $85.25 | $100.00 | +$14.75 | Give $14.75 change |
| $200.00 | $150.00 | -$50.00 | Need $50 more |

<Warning>
The system won't let you complete a payment if the difference is negative (payment incomplete).
</Warning>

## Adding Delivery Information

For orders requiring delivery:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Toggle Delivery">
    Enable the **Is Delivery** checkbox or toggle in the payment dialog.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Delivery Details">
    Fill in the delivery form:
    - **Address**: Street address
    - **City**: City name
    - **State**: State/province
    - **ZIP Code**: Postal code
    - **Phone**: Contact number
    - **Notes**: Delivery instructions (optional)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save with Payment">
    Delivery information is saved with the invoice when you process the payment.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Delivery information is:
- Printed on the invoice (if configured)
- Stored with the sale record
- Viewable in invoice history

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Customer Selector">
    Click the customer dropdown or selection field.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Search for Customer">

## Completing the Payment

<Steps>
  <Step title="Verify All Details">
    Before processing, confirm:
    - ✓ All products and quantities are correct
    - ✓ Discounts applied appropriately
    - ✓ Customer selected (if applicable)
    - ✓ Payment total equals sale total
    - ✓ Payment methods are correct
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Process Payment">
    Press **Enter** or click **Process Payment** button.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Validation">
    The system validates:
    - Cart has products (not empty)
    - Payment total matches sale total (within $0.01 tolerance)
    - All required fields are filled
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Invoice Creation">
    If validation passes:
    - Invoice is created and saved
    - Inventory is updated
    - Stock reservations are finalized
    - Invoice number is generated
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Success Confirmation">
    You'll see:
    - Success message with invoice number
    - Invoice print dialog
    - POS resets for next transaction
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Payment Validation Rules

The system enforces these rules:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Cart Not Empty">
    You cannot process a payment without products in the cart.
    
    **Error**: "Agrega productos antes de procesar"
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Payment Complete">
    Total payments must equal or exceed sale total.
    
    **Error**: "Pago incompleto - Verifica los montos"
    
    **Tolerance**: System allows $0.01 difference for rounding.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Valid Amounts">
    Payment amounts must be:
    - Greater than zero
    - Numeric values
    - Not null/empty
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Stock Available">
    All products must have available stock (already validated when added to cart).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Handling Payment Errors

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="'Pago incompleto' Error">
    **Cause**: Payment total doesn't match sale total
    
    **Solution**:
    - Check each payment amount
    - Use "Fill Exact Amount" for remaining balance
    - Verify no math errors in split payments
    - Check for negative amounts
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Cannot Click Process Payment Button">
    **Cause**: Payment validation failed
    
    **Check**:
    - Is cart empty?
    - Do payments equal total?
    - Are all payment amounts filled in?
    - Is customer required for your business rules?
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Card Payment Not Recording">
    **Cause**: Amount entered doesn't match approved amount
    
    **Solution**:
    - Verify the card terminal approval amount
    - Enter exact approved amount in payment field
    - If void needed, process refund and restart
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Invoice Not Saving">
    **Cause**: Backend error or connectivity issue
    
    **Solution**:
    - Check internet connection
    - Verify backend server is running
    - Check browser console for errors
    - Contact system administrator
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Payment Workflows

### Quick Cash Sale

1. Products already in cart
2. Press **F3**
3. Payment defaults to Cash
4. Enter amount received
5. Note change amount
6. Press **Enter**
7. Give change and receipt

### Card Payment

1. Open payment dialog (F3)
2. Select "Card" payment type
3. Process card on terminal
4. Enter approved amount
5. Press **Enter**
6. Print invoices

### Split Payment (Cash + Card)

1. Open payment (F3)
2. Enter cash amount received
3. Click "Add Payment Method"
4. Select "Card" on second row
5. Process card
6. Enter card amount
7. Verify total payments = sale total
8. Press **Enter**

### Financed with Down Payment

1. Open payment (F3)
2. Enter cash down payment amount
3. Click "Add Payment Method"
4. Select "Financed"
5. Enter financed amount
6. Select number of months
7. Verify totals match
8. Press **Enter**
